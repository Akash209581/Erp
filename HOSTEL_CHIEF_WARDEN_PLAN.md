# SHAMS ERP - Chief Warden Control Tower

This document outlines the high-level administrative tools designed for the **Chief Warden**. This is the "Super Admin" role for the Hostel Module, providing global oversight, final approval authority, and infrastructure management.

---

## 🏰 1. The Control Tower (Global Dashboard)

The Chief Warden does not manage individual rooms; they manage the **entire ecosystem**.

| Metric | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Occupancy Analytics** | Real-time % of beds filled across all Hostels/Campuses. |
| **Financial Pulse** | Total Fees Collected vs. Pending Dues (Synced from Finance). |
| **Operational Health** | Count of Open High-Urgency Complaints vs. Resolved tickets. |
| **Warden Performance** | Tracking how fast Wardens are approving leaves and resolving issues. |

---

## 🏗️ 2. Infrastructure & Resource Management

The Chief Warden is the "Architect" of the hostel system.

*   **Hostel Configuration:** Create and manage Hostels, Blocks, and Rooms.
*   **Asset Master:** Define the global list of assets (e.g., "Standard Bed," "Premium AC," "Study Lamp") that can be assigned to rooms.
*   **Hierarchy Control:** Move blocks or rooms between different management jurisdictions.

---

## 👮 3. Personnel & Administrative Management

### **Warden Assignment (HR Sync)**
*   **Mapping:** Assign specific Wardens (from HR Module) to specific Blocks.
*   **Permissions:** Manage what each Warden can see and do.

### **Final Allocation Approval**
*   **The Request Queue:** A master list of all student "Locked" rooms.
*   **One-Click Finalize:** The Chief Warden gives the final legal approval to move a student into a room.

---

## 🍽️ 4. Mess & Catering Oversight

*   **Menu Approval:** Review and approve the weekly mess menu submitted by catering contractors.
*   **Quality Trends:** View student feedback ratings for meals. If a specific hostel's rating drops, the Chief Warden can take action against the vendor.

---

## 📜 5. Audit & Accountability (The Final Word)

### **Universal Audit Log**
*   A searchable history of **every action** in the system.
*   Example: *"Who changed Student X's room from 101 to 202 at 2 AM?"*
*   The Chief Warden can track exactly which staff member made any change.

### **Override Capability**
*   The Chief Warden can override any Warden's decision (e.g., approving a leave that a Warden rejected).

---

## 🚨 6. Global Emergency Command

*   **Campus-Wide Broadcast:** Unlike a Warden who can only alert their block, the Chief Warden can send an alert to **Every Student** and **Every Staff Member** in the entire university system.

---

## 💡 Chief Warden Logic
1.  **Cross-Module View:** The Chief Warden sees data from Student Master, Finance, and HR in a single "Executive View."
2.  **No Manual Entry:** The Chief Warden focuses on **Approving** and **Monitoring**, while Wardens handle the daily data entry.
3.  **Data-Driven Decisions:** Use feedback and audit logs to improve hostel rules and services.
